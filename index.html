City of Espoo

Smooth everyday life is important in Espoo. Active involvement of residents in the development of our services and our comprehensive co-operation with partners ensure that the development in Espoo is economically, ecologically, socially and culturally sustainable. You will enjoy it!

Espoo is a home for people and enterprises from all over the world. Currently about 15,2 % of the population speak a foreign mother tongue and more than 500 international companies are located in Espoo.
